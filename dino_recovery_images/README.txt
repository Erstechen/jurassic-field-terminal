Dinosaur recovery images — shown on the Embryo Recovered overlay and the Dino DB holographic viewer.

Place one PNG per specimen using these filenames:

  Brachiosaurus.png
  Triceratops.png
  Spinosaurus.png
  Carcharodontosaurus.png
  Tyrannosaurus.png
  Scorpios Rex.png
  Carnotaurus-Toro.png
  Giganotosaurus.png
  Velociraptor.png
  Mutadon.png

Paths are referenced in data/embryos.json and cached by the service worker for offline use.

Note: qr_code_images/ contains printable QR codes for scanning — not dinosaur artwork.
